Who is SEEER





S.E.E.E.R which is specialized in the distribution of electrical equipment. Since 1984, SEEER has made available all of its know-how and perfect knowledge of the products to its large customer base. Thus, S.E.E.E.R supports its customers as closely as possible in the realization of their projects. With more than 700 product references, it offers a wide range of electrical parts in the industrial sector such as electric motors, transformers and electronic cards ...



Our Partners




We benefit from a network of suppliers with whom we have established, over the years, solid partnerships and ties. Thus, we are the exclusive representatives of a number of suppliers such as: Ederfil Becker for enameled wires, Comar condensatori for capacitors, Vonroll Albesiano Dolph's for varnish, Senra for tin.
